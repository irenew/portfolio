Web development

Team:

Eric Leung / Irene Wang

Role:

Web developer intern for SFU Beedie & Segal

About

I worked as a web developer intern at SFU Beedie and Segal for an eight month period. SFU Beedie, and Segal have over eight hundred webpages, and I was tasked to be responsible for maintaining, modifying, and creating all 800 webpages. The major projects that I worked on in the span of my frst work term are the new Beedie, Segal, and EMBA landing/home page, developing webpages for the Beedie undergrad advisors, and Executive Education (Learning Strategies Group). As also being a member of the Marketing and Communication team for Beedie, I'm responsible for creating email newsletters for Career Management Centre, and Alumni Community on a monthly basis.

Achievement

Working along with Eric Leung (Web Service Manager) to implement responsive design on all 800 webpages under the new SFU common look and feel (CLF) guidelines. We were the first faculty to launch the new design (15 minutes after the launch of SFU homepage).
