/ Conversion Globe

Mobile Application

Team:

Ivor Sangala / Irene Wang / Sean Warwick

Role:

Research, UI designer, Developer

Purpose

A mobile application for Nexus 5 that utlizes OpenExchange API to retreive real-time update of conversion rate, and an interactive 3D globe with country touchpoints to change the user experience of a conventional currency converter

Target Audience

Our application would best meet the needs of vacationing tourists who want to frequently convert small amounts of currency.
